About Me

Welcome! I'm currently a teaching assistant professor in the Department of Linguistics at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Previously, I was a lecturer in the Department of Linguistics at the University of California Davis and a postdoctoral fellow in the Department of Cognitive Science at Johns Hopkins University. I completed my Ph.D. in the Department of Linguistics at the University of Southern California in 2018.
