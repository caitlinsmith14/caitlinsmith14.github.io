About Me

Welcome! Beginning this fall, I will be a lecturer in the Department of Linguistics at the University of California Davis. I recently completed a postdoctoral fellowship in the Department of Cognitive Science at Johns Hopkins University. Previously, I was a lecturer in the Department of Linguistics at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, and completed a Ph.D. in the Department of Linguistics at the University of Southern California in 2018.
